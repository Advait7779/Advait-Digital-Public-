What is RCS Bussiness Messaging?
### RCS Business Messaging is an advanced messaging solution that allows businesses to enhance their communication with customers through interactive, rich media messages. It leverages the capabilities of RCS, an upgrade to traditional SMS, to provide a whole host of features such as branding, rich media elements, suggested replies, carousels, and more.
